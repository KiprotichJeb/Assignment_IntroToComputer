Chicken Curry

Home About Meal 1 Meal 2

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Garlic, ginger, oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and cook until golden.
  3. Mix in curry powder and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Nutritional Info (est.)

Calories: 520 | Protein: 30g | Carbs: 20g | Fat: 30g
