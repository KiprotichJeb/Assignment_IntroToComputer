Spaghetti Bolognese

Home About Meal 1 Meal 2

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt, pepper, herbs

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add beef, cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce, simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Combine with spaghetti and serve.

Nutritional Info (est.)

Calories: 450 | Protein: 25g | Carbs: 50g | Fat: 15g
